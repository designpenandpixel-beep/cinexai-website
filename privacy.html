Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

CinexAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at thecinexai.com and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account registration details (name, email, company name)
  • Brand assets and guidelines uploaded to the platform
  • Project briefs, scripts, and production materials
  • Communication and feedback through the platform
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely by third-party providers)

We also collect usage data automatically, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and feature usage patterns to improve platform performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the CinexAI platform
  • Train your dedicated AI Project Manager on your brand
  • Communicate with you about your projects and account
  • Send service updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. Your brand assets and production materials are never shared with other clients or used to train models for other brands.

5. Third-Party Services

CinexAI integrates with third-party AI services (including fal.ai, ElevenLabs, Runway, and others) to provide generation capabilities. Data passed to these services is subject to their respective privacy policies. We only share the minimum data required to fulfil your production requests.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting hello@thecinexai.com. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@thecinexai.com.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at: hello@thecinexai.com
