Cookie Policy
Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our platform.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function. These include session authentication cookies that keep you logged in and security cookies that protect your account. These cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use the platform — which features are most used, where errors occur, and how performance can be improved. Data is aggregated and anonymous.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences, such as your dashboard view preferences and notification settings, so you don't have to reconfigure them each visit.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some features of our platform use third-party services that may set their own cookies, including analytics providers and integrated AI service APIs. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those providers.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using the CinexAI platform. Most browsers allow you to block third-party cookies without affecting essential functionality.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our platform evolves. Changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
